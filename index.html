Juncheng Mu |

I am a first-year Ph.D. student co-advised by Prof. Jiangmiao Pang at Shanghai AI Laboratory and Prof. Huazhe Xu at the Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University. Prior to this, I completed my undergraduate studies at the School of Software, Tsinghua University.

My research interests focus on generalizable robot manipulation, with a particular emphasis on dexterous manipulation. Currently, I also work with Boyi Li at NVIDIA Linning Xu at Shanghai AI Laboratory.
